Blockchain in Internet Of Things

Increasing Intelligent devices and their evolving capabilities to communicate as a Smart Network has given rise to Internet of Things and Industrial Internet of things (particular for the Industries). Prevalent security and communication model in the society and Industries today is centralized which can't scale up and meet the security/authenticity demands of IoT and IIoT.

Blockchain as distributed /tamper-proof /scalable and highly available platform has the potential to cater to IoT and IIoT.

  • Our continuous Innovation AUXIVerse* ( AUXESIS Intelligent Universe ) is a Blockchain Platform to integrate the IoT and IIOT devices and build a distributed ecosystem.
  • Our Already Enterprise Ready Products AUXLEDGER to provide the blockchain fabric and AUXY (a Crypto asset Wallet) and AUXPAY (Crypto Payment system) will bring the needed authenticity and autonomy in IoT and IIoT.
  • Our Enterprise ready Insurance product AUXSecure will bring the insurance/risk mitigation ecosystem needed in IoT and IIoT world.
